About Me

Recruiting built around trust, honesty, and long-term relationships.

I’m Lucas Leitenberger, an Architecture & Engineering recruiter based in Pittsburgh. I partner with firms across Pennsylvania and Ohio, helping them identify and hire talented architects, engineers, interior designers, project managers, and technical leaders.

My goal has always been simple: understand what people truly want, communicate honestly, and create connections that improve both a firm and a professional’s long-term future. I believe recruiting should feel personal, responsive, and grounded in real relationships—not transactions.

Outside of work, I’m married to my high school crush, and together we are raising our two little girls in the Pittsburgh area. Family is at the center of everything I do and has shaped the patience, perspective, and long-term mindset I bring to my work every day.

About Lucas

Relationships first. Recruiting second.

For the past six years, I have had the opportunity to help architects, designers, and engineering professionals take the next step in their careers while partnering with firms across Pennsylvania and Ohio to build exceptional teams.

What I enjoy most about recruiting is not simply filling positions—it is building long-term relationships. The best partnerships come from understanding what truly motivates people, whether that means more meaningful project work, stronger leadership, better work-life balance, or new opportunities for growth.

Because I work exclusively within the Architecture & Engineering industry, I have developed a deep understanding of the firms, project types, and personalities that make each opportunity unique. My goal is always to provide honest guidance, thoughtful introductions, and a recruiting experience built on trust—not pressure.

Today, I partner with Green Key Resources alongside an outstanding recruiting team, helping firms throughout Pennsylvania, Ohio, and beyond identify exceptional talent while helping candidates find organizations where they can build meaningful, long-term careers.

About Lucas

Recruiting is personal to me.

I have built my career around helping architects, engineers, interior designers, and firm leaders make decisions that genuinely improve their work and their lives. My approach is direct, honest, and focused on long-term fit—not simply filling a role.

I live in the Pittsburgh area with my wife, who was my high school crush, and our two little girls. Family sits at the center of everything I do, and it has shaped how I work: with patience, accountability, empathy, and a genuine interest in the people behind every career move.

Over the last several years, I have partnered with architecture and engineering firms throughout Pennsylvania and Ohio, helping them hire emerging professionals, experienced project leaders, technical specialists, and firm leadership.

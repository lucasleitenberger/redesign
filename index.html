A little about me

Recruiting has always been about relationships for me.

Over the past six years, I have built partnerships throughout Pennsylvania and Ohio's architecture and engineering community. Along the way, I have learned that the best opportunities rarely begin with a job board. They begin with an honest conversation.

I do not believe in pushing people toward opportunities that are not right for them. My role is to listen first, understand what matters, and help firms and professionals make decisions they will feel good about years from now.

Whether I am helping a growing firm make a critical hire or speaking with someone who is quietly considering a new chapter, my goal is the same: create lasting relationships built on trust.
